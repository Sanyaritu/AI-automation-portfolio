# 🌸 BloomAssist AI

An AI-powered customer support assistant built with **n8n** that helps businesses automate customer interactions using AI.

## 📌 Project Overview

BloomAssist AI is designed for e-commerce businesses to provide instant, intelligent customer support.

The assistant can answer frequently asked questions, maintain conversation context, and is being expanded into a complete AI customer support platform with document retrieval, email automation, and human escalation.

## 💡 Business Use Case

BloomAssist AI helps businesses reduce repetitive customer support work by answering common customer queries instantly.

Example questions include:

- What is your return policy?
- How long does shipping take?
- Do you offer international shipping?
- What are your customer support hours?
