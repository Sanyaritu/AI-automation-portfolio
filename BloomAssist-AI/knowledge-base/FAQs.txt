Bloom Boutique Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How can I track my order?
A: Once your order is shipped, you'll receive an email with a tracking link and tracking number.

Q: How long does shipping take?
A: Standard shipping takes 3–5 business days, while express shipping usually takes 1–2 business days.

Q: Do you offer international shipping?
A: Yes. We currently ship to selected countries. Shipping costs are calculated during checkout.

Q: Can I cancel my order?
A: Orders can be cancelled before they are dispatched. After dispatch, customers must request a return after delivery.

Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: We accept UPI, credit cards, debit cards, net banking, and major digital wallets.

Q: How can I contact customer support?
A: You can reach us by email at support@bloomboutique.com or during business hours using our live chat service.

Q: How long do refunds take?
A: Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days after the returned product passes quality inspection.
