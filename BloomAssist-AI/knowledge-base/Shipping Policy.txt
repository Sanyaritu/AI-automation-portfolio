Bloom Boutique Shipping Policy

At Bloom Boutique, we aim to deliver every order quickly and safely. Standard shipping within India usually takes 3–5 business days from the date of dispatch. Express shipping is available for selected locations and generally arrives within 1–2 business days. Delivery timelines may vary during festivals, public holidays, severe weather conditions, or other unforeseen circumstances.

Orders are processed within 24 hours after successful payment confirmation. Once your order has been dispatched, you will receive an email containing your tracking number and shipment details. Customers can track their orders using the provided tracking link.

We offer free standard shipping on all orders above ₹999. Orders below ₹999 are charged a flat shipping fee of ₹99. International shipping is available for selected countries. Shipping charges and estimated delivery dates for international orders are calculated during checkout based on the destination.

If your package is delayed by more than seven business days, please contact our customer support team with your order number. We will investigate the shipment with our delivery partner and provide updates as quickly as possible.
