Privacy Policy

Bloom Boutique values customer privacy and is committed to protecting personal information. We collect only the information necessary to process orders, improve customer experience, and provide customer support.

Information we may collect includes your name, email address, phone number, shipping address, billing address, and order history. Payment information is processed securely through trusted payment providers and is never stored on our servers.

Your personal information is used only for order processing, delivery updates, customer support, promotional communications (with your consent), and improving our services.

We never sell customer data to third parties. Information may only be shared with trusted logistics partners, payment processors, or service providers when required to complete your order.

Customers may request updates or deletion of their personal information by contacting support@bloomboutique.com. We continuously maintain security measures to protect customer information from unauthorized access or misuse.
