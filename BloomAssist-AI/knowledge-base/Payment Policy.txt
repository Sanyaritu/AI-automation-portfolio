Payment Policy

Bloom Boutique provides secure payment options to ensure a safe shopping experience for all customers. We accept UPI, Visa, MasterCard, RuPay debit cards, credit cards, net banking, and major digital wallets.

Cash on Delivery (COD) is available for eligible locations within India for orders up to ₹10,000. A small convenience fee may apply depending on the delivery location.

All online payments are processed using secure encrypted payment gateways. Bloom Boutique does not store customers' debit card, credit card, or banking information on its servers.

If your payment fails but the amount is deducted from your account, it is usually reversed automatically by your bank within 3–7 business days. If the issue persists, please contact your bank first and then reach out to our customer support team with your transaction details.

Invoices are automatically generated after successful payment and are sent to the registered email address.
