Bloom Boutique Size Guide

Choosing the right size helps ensure a comfortable fit and reduces the need for returns. All measurements listed below are in centimeters and represent body measurements, not garment measurements.

Women's Size Chart

XS
Bust: 80–84 cm
Waist: 62–66 cm
Hips: 86–90 cm

S
Bust: 84–88 cm
Waist: 66–70 cm
Hips: 90–94 cm

M
Bust: 88–94 cm
Waist: 70–76 cm
Hips: 94–100 cm

L
Bust: 94–100 cm
Waist: 76–82 cm
Hips: 100–106 cm

XL
Bust: 100–106 cm
Waist: 82–88 cm
Hips: 106–112 cm

If your measurements fall between two sizes, we recommend choosing the larger size for a more relaxed fit or the smaller size for a fitted look. Product pages also include model measurements and fit descriptions to help you choose the correct size.

If you need assistance selecting the right size, our customer support team is happy to help before you place your order.
