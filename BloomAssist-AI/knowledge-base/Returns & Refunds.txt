Bloom Boutique Returns & Refunds Policy

Customer satisfaction is our priority. If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, you may request a return within 30 days from the date of delivery. Returned products must be unused, unwashed, undamaged, and include all original tags and packaging.

To initiate a return, log into your account and submit a return request, or contact our customer support team with your order number. Once the request is approved, our delivery partner will arrange a pickup where available. Customers in locations without pickup service may be asked to ship the product to our return center.

After receiving the returned item, our quality team will inspect it. Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days after the inspection is successfully completed. Refunds are credited to the original payment method.

Products purchased during clearance sales, gift cards, and personalized items cannot be returned unless they arrive damaged or defective. If you receive an incorrect, damaged, or defective product, please report the issue within 48 hours of delivery along with clear photographs so we can provide a replacement or refund.
